Info

Though he made his first WWE appearance in 1990 when he drove The Honky Tonk Man and Greg Valentine to the ring in a pink Cadillac at WrestleMania VI, Diamond Dallas Page became most well-known for the Diamond Cutter, his devastating finishing maneuver that helped him earn numerous championships throughout his career.
