Info

Competing in nearly 30 countries, Sami Zayn has gathered more experience than most WWE Superstars. His cheerful demeanor and optimistic attitude mask the vigor of this former NXT champion. "The Underdog from the Underground" gained this appropriate nickname after collecting dozens of title reigns in organizations all over the world, and like his matches with Braun Strowman, Sami Zayn doesn't plan on stopping any time soon.
