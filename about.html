About

WWE Champions Guide was created for the novice and hardcore players alike. It helps you become a better champion at WWE Champions mobile game.

This guide was created by a fan of the game, and is not associated in any way with Scopely, Kung Fu Factory, or WWE.

WWE: Champions is the new puzzle RPG that pits the WWE’s greatest Superstars against each other in the ultimate quest for stardom. Create and customize your team of WWE Superstars, and brawl through generations of the WWE’s greatest. Think you’ve got the best team? Prove it! Send your team of WWE Superstars into the ring and test your fighting skills. Match gems across the board and make your move. Win, and your champions will live on as immortals. Lose, and face defeat at your opponent’s hands. Come join a community of fellow WWE fans and start battling for reputation, power and ultimate bragging rights in WWE: Champions!
